Our new brand identity
Our new brand identity is finally here and we can't wait for you to see it. Inspired by timeless colours, vintage patterns, and bold personalities - we have taken these elements from fashion history and used them to build a modern brand, for a digital world. The intention is never to look like a fashion label ourselves, but instead, a representation of fashion communities and the people that make fashion great.
