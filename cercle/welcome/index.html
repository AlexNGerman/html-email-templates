It is a pleasure to welcome you to our CERCLE community.

CERCLE is the innovative style rental platform for conscious individuals who want to consume less and access more. It is also the first philanthropic platform focusing on giving back. We enable lenders to leverage their under-utilised wardrobes to raise funds for a charity dear to their heart.

With 10 years living in capital cities including Beijing, New York, Brussels and of course, London, I’ve learnt the value of a ‘less is more’ approach to style. Dressing only from two suitcases and borrowing from close friends and family made sharing as pleasurable as owning. That’s the experience I’d like to share with our amazing community through CERCLE, which takes its name from the French phrase ‘cercle d’amis’ or ‘circle of friends’. It also references the circular economy of Style Sharing and the environmental benefits.

We love the idea of living a minimalist life but with maximum access. Style sharing does this. It’s better for our souls and better for the earth. It is our belief that a well-curated life deserves to be shared.

Myself and the team would be delighted to invite you for a Welcome Styling Session at L’Appartement CERCLE and provide you with some tailored recommendations.

I really look forward to seeing you - hopefully in your next shared piece.
